Kids Story App

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 3, 2026

Introduction

Kids Story App ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

Important: This App is designed for users aged 13 years and older. We take privacy very seriously and do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide to Us

When you create an account and use our App, we collect the following information:

1. Account Information:

  • Email address (required for account creation and authentication)
  • Display name (chosen by you, visible to other users)
  • Age (required, must be 13+ years)
  • Password (encrypted and stored securely by Firebase Authentication)

2. Content You Create:

  • Stories (title, text content, images)
  • Comments on stories
  • Reactions to stories (likes, hearts, etc.)
  • Tags and genre selections
  • Profile information (optional avatar image)

3. User-Generated Content:

  • Images uploaded with stories (stored in Firebase Cloud Storage)
  • Story drafts (saved privately until published)

Information Automatically Collected

1. Device Information:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Unique device identifiers (for Firebase services)

2. Usage Information:

  • Stories you read, create, or interact with
  • Search queries
  • App features you use

3. Technical Information:

  • IP address (collected by Firebase services)
  • Crash reports and error logs (for app improvement)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. Provide and Maintain the App:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Authenticate your login
  • Store and display your stories
  • Enable social features (comments, reactions)

2. Improve the App:

  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Analyze usage patterns
  • Enhance user experience

3. Ensure Safety:

  • Moderate content for appropriateness
  • Handle user reports
  • Protect against abuse and inappropriate content

4. Communication:

  • Send password reset emails (if requested)
  • Respond to support requests

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

Firebase (Google)

  • Firebase Authentication: Handles user authentication and stores encrypted passwords
  • Cloud Firestore: Stores user profiles, stories, comments, and reactions
  • Cloud Storage: Stores images uploaded with stories
  • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Firebase may collect:

  • Device information
  • IP addresses
  • Usage analytics
  • Crash reports

Image Picker Library

  • react-native-image-picker: Accesses your device's photo library to select images for stories
  • This library only accesses images you explicitly choose to upload
  • No images are accessed without your permission

Data Storage and Security

Where Your Data is Stored

  • User Accounts: Stored in Firebase Authentication (Google's secure servers)
  • Stories and Content: Stored in Firebase Cloud Firestore (Google's secure servers)
  • Images: Stored in Firebase Cloud Storage (Google's secure servers)
  • Location: Data is stored on Google's servers, which may be located in various regions

Security Measures

  • All passwords are encrypted using industry-standard hashing algorithms
  • Data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Access to data is restricted through Firebase security rules
  • Regular security audits and updates

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

  • Age Verification: We require users to be at least 13 years old to create an account.
  • No Knowing Collection: If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a user under the age of 13, we will take steps to delete such information from our records immediately.
  • Parental Inquiries: If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at mmm.brame@gmail.com.
  • No Targeted Advertising: We do not display third-party advertisements or share user data with advertisers.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  1. Access: You can view your account information and stories within the App.
  2. Correction: You can update your display name, age, and profile information in the App settings.
  3. Deletion:
    • You can delete individual stories through the App
    • You can request account deletion by contacting us
    • When you delete your account, we will delete your personal information. Published stories may remain visible to other users as part of the community content.
  4. Data Portability: You can request a copy of your data by contacting us.
  5. Opt-Out: You can stop using the App and delete your account at any time.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your information with advertisers
  • Use your information for marketing purposes outside the App

We MAY share information:

  • With service providers (Firebase/Google) who help us operate the App
  • If required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • In case of a business transfer (merger, acquisition, etc.)

Content Visibility

Important Information About Published Stories:

  • When you publish a story, it becomes visible to all users of the App
  • Your display name is shown with your published stories
  • Comments and reactions on your stories are visible to all users
  • Published stories cannot be made private after publishing
  • You can delete your published stories at any time

Draft Stories:

  • Draft stories are private and only visible to you
  • Drafts are stored securely and not accessible to other users

Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your information as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: We delete account information within 30 days of account deletion
  • Published Content: Published stories may remain in the App even after account deletion, as they are part of the community content
  • Backup Data: Some data may remain in backup systems for up to 90 days

Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with applicable privacy laws and principles, including:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) principles
  • Google Play Store and App Store privacy requirements
